THE BEST MOVIES I WATCHED SO FAR (THIS 2025)

My Top 3 so far on the year 2025

Senior Year (2010)

Jerrold Tarog

Synopsis: Senior Year is a glimpse into the lives of ten students at St. Frederick’s Academy as they struggle through the final months before graduation. Features a cast of real high school students.

Rating: ★★★★★

Confessional (2007)

Jerrold Tarog

Synopsis: On a trip to Cebu to document the Sinulog Festival, small time filmmaker Ryan Pastor meets Lito Caliso, a former mayor who decides to confess in front of Ryan's camera all the crimes he committed while in office.

Rating: ★★★★★

Napoleon Dynamite (2004)

Jared Hess

A listless and alienated teenager helps his new friend win the class presidency in their small western high school, while dealing with his bizarre family life back home.

Rating: ★★★★☆
